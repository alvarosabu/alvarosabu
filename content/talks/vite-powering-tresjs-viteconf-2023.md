# How Vite Is Powering the TresJS Ecosystem

Explore how Vite powers the TresJS ecosystem, enabling fast development and optimal performance for Vue-based 3D applications.

This 11-minute talk dives into the technical foundations of TresJS and how Vite's lightning-fast HMR (Hot Module Replacement) and optimized build process enhance the developer experience when working with 3D content in Vue.

The presentation showcases how Vite's architecture makes it the perfect tool for building complex 3D applications with TresJS, providing instant feedback during development and optimized production builds.
