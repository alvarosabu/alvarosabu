# Bring the Magic of 3D to Your Vue Applications with TresJS

TresJS is a Vue custom renderer that enables you to craft immersive 3D experiences declaratively using ThreeJS components. Discover how Tres breaks down the barrier between developers and the 3D world using their favorite framework.

This 9-minute lightning talk demonstrates how TresJS makes 3D development accessible for the Vue.js community without requiring extensive knowledge of complex 3D concepts or WebGL.

The talk was presented in an observatory setting at JSNation US 2024, which took place in a unique planetarium venue in New York.
