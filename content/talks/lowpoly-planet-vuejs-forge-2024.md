# How to Create a 3D Low Poly Planet Scene with TresJS

An intro to creating a 3D low poly planet scene with TresJS. Learn how to export models from Blender and integrate them into your Vue applications.

This workshop-style session features a low poly planet exported from Blender, demonstrating practical techniques for working with 3D assets in TresJS. The session covers basic concepts, using the Cientos library, loading GLTF models with useGLTF, and integrating Blender exports.

You can view the live demo of the Low Poly Planet at the TresJS Lab.
