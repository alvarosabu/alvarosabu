# Shiny, Pretty and Post-processed TresJS Effects for Visuals

Explore advanced post-processing effects in TresJS to create stunning, shiny, and visually impressive 3D experiences in Vue applications.

This talk dives into the world of visual effects and post-processing in TresJS, showcasing how to enhance your 3D scenes with advanced rendering techniques including shaders, bloom effects, depth of field, and other visual enhancements that make your 3D applications truly stand out.

Learn how to leverage the TresJS ecosystem to create professional-grade visual effects without deep WebGL knowledge.
